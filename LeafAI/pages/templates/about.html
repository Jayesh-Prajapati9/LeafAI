About LeafAI

LeafAI is an AI-powered web application designed to help farmers, researchers, and plant lovers detect plant diseases quickly and accurately using just an image. By combining the power of Machine Learning and Django, LeafAI aims to make agriculture smarter, healthier, and more sustainable.

🌱 Our Mission

Every year, plant diseases cause billions of dollars in crop losses worldwide. Farmers often struggle with late detection, limited resources, or lack of expert access. LeafAI’s mission is to bridge the gap between technology and agriculture by giving anyone the ability to diagnose plant health issues instantly, helping reduce crop damage and improve global food security.

⚙️ Technology Behind LeafAI

Machine Learning

LeafAI uses deep learning models trained on plant disease datasets. These models analyze uploaded leaf images to detect disease symptoms with high accuracy.

Django Framework

The web app is built with Django, ensuring a secure, scalable, and maintainable platform for users across the globe.

TailwindCSS

A modern and responsive UI designed with TailwindCSS, making LeafAI easy to use on desktop and mobile devices.

Database

User predictions and image uploads are stored in a database for easy retrieval, enabling personalized dashboards and history tracking.

🌍 Who Can Use LeafAI?

  • Farmers: Detect diseases early and reduce crop losses.
  • Researchers: Analyze plant health patterns and disease outbreaks.
  • Students: Learn how AI can be applied in agriculture.
  • Gardeners: Keep houseplants and small gardens healthy.

🚀 Future Goals

LeafAI is just the beginning. In the future, we plan to add:

  • Real-time disease detection via mobile app.
  • Multi-language support for farmers worldwide.
  • Integration with agricultural advisory services.
  • Community-driven dataset expansion for new plant species.
